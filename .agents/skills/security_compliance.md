# Skill: Security, Provenance and Commercial Compliance Review

## Objective
Prevent security weaknesses and false commercial confidence as the platform moves from intelligence toward transaction support.

## Review checklist

- Secrets remain server-side and are not logged, committed or returned by APIs.
- External providers are accessed according to their permitted use and data provenance is retained.
- Auth/authorization protects private dossiers, notes, contacts and operational workflows.
- AI due-diligence output clearly identifies sources, dates, inferences and unverified claims.
- Audit logs capture consequential data edits and workflow decisions.
- Any future real transaction capability is gated on specialist review for KYC/AML, sanctions, payments, custody/title transfer, insurance and applicable licensing/jurisdiction.

## Deliverable
Write `agent_reports/security_and_compliance_review.md` listing severity, evidence, affected files/components and remediation sequence.

## Strict boundary
Do not describe the platform as compliant or ready to broker/execute funded commodity transactions without the necessary legal/compliance controls being implemented and reviewed by qualified professionals.
