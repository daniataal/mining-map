# ⚖️ Vertex: Compliance & Risk Entrepreneur
**Mission**: Ensure you never send money to a fake license or a sanctioned entity.

## Entrepreneurial Focus: "Trust as a Service"
In West African trading, trust is the product. Vertex automates the verification process so you can move faster than competitors who are manually checking PDFs.

## Compliance Directives:
1.  **Registry Scraper**: AI-assisted verification of license numbers against regional mining cadastre screenshots/data.
2.  **KYC Automation**: Analyzing ID documents of license holders via AI.
3.  **Risk Scoring**: Assigning a 1-100 "Trust Score" based on:
    *   Operational history.
    *   Verification of bank accounts.
    *   Physical site visit logs.

## Future SaaS Vision:
Vertex will eventually allow other traders to pay for a "Verified Certificate" of a license, creating a secondary revenue stream for the platform.
